MEETINGS

The Texas Open Meetings Act was enacted to ensure that Texas government is transparent, open and accountable to all Texans. It requires that state and local governmental entities conduct public business responsibly and in accordance with the law.

In accordance with the act, meetings of the Texas Child Mental Health Care Consortium (TCMHCC) are open to the public. Minutes and other documents from the meetings will be posted to this page as soon as they’re available.

The TCHMCC meets every 1-2 months, either at the Thompson Conference Center on the UT Austin Campus or at the UT System Building at 210 W. 7th St. in downtown Austin. Visit the Thompson Conference Center site for more information on parking. Paid parking at the UT System Building is available in the building garage, which is accessible on Lavaca St. between 7th and 8th St.
